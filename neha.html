About Neha Enterprises

With decades of combined experience in the footwear industry, Neha Enterprises was founded on the principles of quality, durability, and reliability. We are a leading manufacturer of shoe soles, providing robust and stylish foundations for shoe brands across the globe.

Our state-of-the-art factory is equipped with modern machinery and operated by a team of skilled professionals dedicated to perfection. We believe that every great pair of shoes starts with a great sole, and we are committed to being the best at creating them.

Our Core Services

We provide specialized services to complete the shoe manufacturing process.

Sole Manufacturing

Producing a wide variety of high-grade soles (TPR, PU, EVA, Rubber) using precision molding techniques for maximum comfort and longevity.

Durable Sole Stitching

Our expert stitching service ensures a secure and lasting bond between the sole and the upper, using high-tensile threads for superior durability.

Fitter on Upper Shoes

We precisely attach and fit the manufactured sole to your shoe uppers, ensuring a perfect, comfortable, and aesthetically pleasing final product.
